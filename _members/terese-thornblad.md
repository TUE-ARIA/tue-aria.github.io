Terese Thornblad is a PhD candidate in electrical engineering at Eindhoven University of Technology, where her research focuses on AI for healthcare. With a background in medical physics and clinical experience as a licensed medical physicist in Sweden, she works on CT-based methods for tumor detection and diagnosis, bridging technical innovation with medical application.
