Tim Boers is an engineer and researcher specializing in high-tech systems and advanced imaging technologies. He studied at the University of Twente and pursued his PhD at Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e), where he deepened his expertise in complex technical systems and applied research. Alongside his academic work, Tim is currently employed at ASML, contributing to cutting-edge developments in the semiconductor sector.
