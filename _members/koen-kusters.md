Koen Kusters is an AI research engineer at ScreenPoint Medical and recently completed a PhD in Medical Computer Vision and AI. He earned his BSc and MSc in Electrical Engineering from Eindhoven University of Technology, where he conducted doctoral research on medical computer vision and image processing for endoscopy applications, with a focus on AI for cancer detection and diagnosis.
